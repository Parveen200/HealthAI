Health Check

Ensure your well-being with our comprehensive health check-up packages tailored to detect and prevent illnesses at an early stage. Our packages include:

Heart Disease

Early detection of heart disease is vital for effective prevention and management. Our heart health check-up packages include comprehensive assessments such as cholesterol levels, blood pressure monitoring, and risk factor evaluation.

Kidney Disease

Kidney health is essential for overall well-being. Our check-up packages include tests to evaluate kidney function, such as serum creatinine, blood urea nitrogen (BUN), and urine analysis.

Liver Disease

Protect your liver health with our thorough check-up packages. We offer tests to monitor liver function, including liver enzyme levels, bilirubin, and imaging studies.

Breast Cancer

Early detection of breast cancer is essential for successful treatment. Our check-up packages include mammograms, breast ultrasounds, and clinical breast exams. Regular screenings and timely intervention can improve outcomes and enhance overall breast health.

Diabetes

Our diabetes check-up packages provide crucial monitoring of blood glucose, A1c levels, and screening for complications, along with personalized lifestyle guidance for effective management.
